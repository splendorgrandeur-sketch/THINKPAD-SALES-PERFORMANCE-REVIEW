### 📈 Project Overview
An interactive **Microsoft Excel Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard** built to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence. Leveraging Power Query, Pivot Tables, and Data Visualization techniques, this project identifies revenue drivers, profitability trends, customer behavior patterns, and sales team performance to support data-driven, strategic decision-making.

---
### 🎯 Problem Statement: Bridging the Sales Intelligence Gap
Despite consistent daily transaction volume, the business lacked a centralized mechanism to visualize performance across fragmented regions, diverse customer segments, and varying product categories. Stakeholders were previously unable to quickly answer critical questions such as:

* **Regional Performance:** Why is profitability concentrated in specific states like Lagos and Abuja, and how can these successes be replicated?
* **Customer Value:** Which demographics, such as Young Adults, provide the highest value and warrant focused marketing efforts?
* **Profit Volatility:** What are the root causes behind the shifts shown in the annual profit trend analysis?
* **Operational Efficiency:** How do different payment preferences (Cash vs. Digital) impact sales velocity and overhead?

### 📊 Key Business Findings
* **Total Revenue:** Generated ₦22.34M in total revenue.
* **Profitability:** Achieved ₦2.26M profit.
* **Product Performance:** Phones accounted for the largest share of sales, and iPhone emerged as the top-selling product.
* **Market Reach:** Lagos and Abuja were the strongest markets.
* **Customer Insights:** Young Adults represented the most valuable customer segment.
* **Payment Trends:** Cash remained the dominant payment method.

### 💡 Strategic Business Recommendations
* **Profitability:** Improve profit margins through focused cost optimization.
* **Focus:** Increase focus on high-performing products.
* **Growth:** Expand successful sales strategies across additional states.
* **Modernization:** Promote digital payment adoption.
* **Strategy:** Strengthen customer segmentation and targeting.
